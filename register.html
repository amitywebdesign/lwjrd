Skate with us.

Long Wharf JRD is a brand new skater-led junior roller derby program serving New Haven County, Connecticut. Our founding cohort is forming now.

Roller derby is a full-contact sport played on quad roller skates and one of the most dynamic and welcoming sports in the world. It is fast, physical, and deeply strategic, and it rewards athletes who think as hard as they hit. You do not need any prior experience to join us.

Long Wharf JRD is coached and captained by experienced junior derby athletes who have competed at the regional level and are passionate about growing the sport in their own community. Skaters progress through skill levels at their own pace, starting with foundational skating and safety fundamentals and working toward full-contact sanctioned competition under the Junior Roller Derby Association ruleset. Every skater begins at the same starting point regardless of background, and every skater advances when they are ready.

What to bring to your first practice

  • Quad roller skates
  • Certified helmet
  • Knee pads
  • Elbow pads
  • Wrist guards
  • Mouthguard
  • Water bottle
  • Comfortable athletic clothing

Inline skates are not permitted under JRDA rules. If you are still putting your gear together, register anyway and reach out — we can point you in the right direction and connect you with our recommended retailers.

Register below

A parent or guardian must complete and sign all sections before any skater participates in on-skate activity. If you have any questions before filling this out, email longwharfjrd@gmail.com and we will get back to you quickly.

What happens after you register

We get in touch

Within a few days of receiving your registration, you'll get a confirmation email with practice details, the parent and skater code-of-conduct packet, and any final paperwork.

You come to your first practice

Show up in gear ready to skate. We'll do a brief intake, an initial skills check, and slot you into the right tier. Bring a parent or guardian who can sign in-person paperwork.

You start skating

From there you're on the floor with us, working through the tier system at your own pace. Welcome to Long Wharf.
