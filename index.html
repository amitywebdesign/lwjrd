What we are

A junior roller derby league for the kids of southern Connecticut.

Long Wharf Junior Roller Derby is a skater-led competitive program serving athletes ages 12 to 17 from across New Haven County. Our travel team is the Amity Mariners.

Roller derby is full-contact. It rewards athletes who think as hard as they hit. We are not a recreational program. We are building skaters who can compete at the highest levels of junior derby and earn their way onto adult rosters when they age up.

Southern Connecticut has not had a competitive junior derby program before. We are changing that.

01 / Ages

12 to 17

Open to any skater in the age range, regardless of derby experience. We assess and place every skater individually.

02 / Cadence

Four sessions a year

Summer, Fall, Winter, Spring. Friday evenings in New Haven County. Structured progression from foundations to full contact.

03 / Affiliation

JRDA-aligned

Operating under the rules of the Junior Roller Derby Association with structured skill assessments and safety standards.

04 / Culture

Skater-led

Coached and captained by experienced junior derby athletes who have competed at the regional level. Our skaters set the standard.

Ready to skate?

If you can skate and you are ready to work, we will take care of the rest. Bring quad skates, a certified helmet, knee pads, elbow pads, wrist guards, and a mouthguard. Bring everything else with you.
